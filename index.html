The Two Worlds of Kowloon Walled City

-Historical Enclave and Heavy Band-

Many people do not know of the original Kowloon Walled City,
as it existed for decades on the periphery of Hong Kong,
and was demolished in the early 1990's.

Many people also may not know of the other Kowloon Walled City either,
a post-metal band based out of the Bay Area.

This website attempts to connect the two disparate ideas,
as one (the band) appears to be heavily influenced by the other (the enclave).
